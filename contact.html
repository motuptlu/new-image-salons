FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

While we welcome walk-ins based on availability, we highly recommend booking an appointment to ensure your preferred time and stylist. You can book online, via WhatsApp, or by calling us.

What is your cancellation policy?

We request at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. This allows us to offer the time slot to other clients. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a cancellation fee.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), and debit cards. We also accept contactless payments including Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer complimentary consultations for all services. This is a great opportunity to discuss your hair goals, ask questions, and get expert advice before committing to a service.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have ample free parking available in our plaza. The salon is also wheelchair accessible with convenient entry.

What products do you use?

We use only premium, professional-grade products from trusted brands including Redken, Olaplex, Moroccan Oil, and other high-quality lines. All our products are safe and deliver exceptional results.
