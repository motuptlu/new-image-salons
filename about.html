Our Story

Where Passion Meets Perfection

Founded in 2016, New Image Hair Salon has grown from a small dream into Calgary's most trusted destination for premium hair care. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a space where artistry, expertise, and exceptional service come together.

Today, we're proud to serve the vibrant Cornerstone community and beyond, offering cutting-edge techniques, luxurious treatments, and personalized care that transforms not just hair, but confidence and self-expression.
